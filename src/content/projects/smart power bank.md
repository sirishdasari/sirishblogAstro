**Project Overview:**
The Smart Power Bank is a cutting-edge Tech designed to meet the demands of modern users. With fast charging capabilities and multi-device support, it ensures that users can stay powered up on the go.

> Touch Screen Technology: The power bank features a sleek touchscreen interface that allows users to easily monitor battery levels, select charging modes, and customize settings.
> Fast Charging: Equipped with the latest fast-charging technology, the Smart Power Bank can quickly recharge devices, minimizing downtime and maximizing convenience.
> Multi-Device Support: The power bank is designed to charge multiple devices simultaneously, making it ideal for users with various gadgets such as smartphones and accessories.
> Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Connectivity: The Smart Power Bank can connect to a companion app via Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, allowing users to monitor charging status, set reminders, and receive notifications.
